#EFEFEF - Background

1. Primary Colors – Structure & Foundation
Almost Black — #0E0E10

Use for:

App header / navigation bar / sidebar

Key containers for sensitive data (balances, portfolio summaries)

Primary headings and titles (if you want maximum contrast)

Why:
Dark, almost-black backgrounds exude authority and seriousness, making your app feel institutional.

Warm Gray — #3A3A3C

Use for:

Secondary containers / cards / side panels

Subheadings and labels

Body text for charts or tables (for better readability than pure black)

Why:
Warm gray keeps things less harsh than pure black, improving readability and user comfort.

2. Secondary Colors – Support & Hierarchy
Stone — #9A9A9A

Use for:

Form labels, helper text, and metadata

Table borders, dividers, subtle lines

Disabled buttons and inactive icons

Why:
Stone is muted and unobtrusive, giving a sense of structure without distraction.

Off-White — #EFEFEF

Use for:

Main app background

Card backgrounds and light panels

Input fields and modal overlays

Why:
Keeps the interface airy and readable, perfect for data-heavy UIs.

3. Accent Colors – Emphasis & Premium Feel
Platinum — #BFC5C9

Use for:

Highlighted text (metrics, key values)

Borders around important cards or buttons

Subtle hover effects

Icon backgrounds

Why:
It’s premium but subtle, signaling importance without loudness.

Deep Forest — #1E4D3A

Use for:

Positive indicators (balances, profits, trends)

Primary call-to-action buttons

Active tabs, selected states, or key icons

Why:
Dark green evokes wealth, growth, and financial confidence, ideal for actionable or positive data points.

4. Status Colors (Optional)

Error / Negative: Muted red (#B24A4A) for failed transactions or declines

Warning / Neutral alerts: Amber (#D9A441) sparingly

Use only when necessary — Executive Minimal is calm and controlled.

5. Buttons & Interactive Elements
Type	Background	Text	Use
Primary	Deep Forest #1E4D3A	Off-White #EFEFEF	Main actions like Transfer, Invest
Secondary	Off-White #EFEFEF	Almost Black #0E0E10	Secondary actions, navigation
Disabled	Stone #9A9A9A	Warm Gray #3A3A3C	Unavailable actions
Destructive	Muted Red	Off-White	Delete / cancel actions
6. Text Color Hierarchy

Headings: Almost Black #0E0E10

Subheadings / Labels: Warm Gray #3A3A3C

Body Text: Stone #9A9A9A

Highlights / Key Metrics: Platinum #BFC5C9 or Deep Forest #1E4D3A

7. Charts & Data Visualization

Background: Off-White #EFEFEF

Primary Data: Deep Forest #1E4D3A

Secondary / Comparative Data: Warm Gray #3A3A3C

Borders / Gridlines: Stone #9A9A9A

Highlight / Trend Up: Deep Forest #1E4D3A

Trend Down: Muted Red (optional)

Keep it low-saturation — minimal, elegant, professional.
